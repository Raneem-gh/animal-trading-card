Striped Dolphin

Striped dolphin is a marine mammal:it breathes air, is warm-blooded, gives birth and produces milk to feed its children.

  • Class: Mammels
  • Avarage length (Adult): 2.6 - 2.4 m.
  • Avarage lifespan: up to 60 years.
  • Habitat: warm-temperate and tropical seas.

Dolphins are known of their intelligence and communication skills. striped dolphin swims in large groups. Their diet consists of fish, squid, cephalopods and octopus.
